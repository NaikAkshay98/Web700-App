About Me

I am currently pursuing a Database Application Developer program at Seneca College in Toronto, Canada, building on my prior education in Business Analytics from the same institution. I completed my Bachelor's in Computer Engineering from Mumbai University, India, where I developed a strong foundation in technical skills and problem-solving. In terms of professional experience, I have been working as a Printer/Associate at Zenan Glass in Toronto, where I oversee printing orders, manage shipping logistics, and ensure smooth operations on the factory floor. Previously, I worked as a Test Analyst at Tata Consultancy Services Ltd in Mumbai, where I contributed to project management and conducted thorough testing and reporting. In addition to my professional pursuits, I have actively engaged in volunteer work, including serving as a College Food Pantry Representative Volunteer, where I played a key role in food distribution and administrative support. I have also contributed to environmental initiatives, such as Beach Cleaning and Tree Plantation activities in Mumbai, aimed at fostering a cleaner and more sustainable ecosystem. I am passionate about leveraging my technical expertise and interpersonal skills to contribute positively to both the professional and social realms. I value teamwork, attention to detail, and a commitment to continuous learning and growth.
